Quality politics

Establish alliances with suppliers and customers, providing the latter with a timely service tailored to your needs and expectations, ensuring good prices and quality of excellence in the products and services offered, helping us to consolidate and continuously improve the Quality Management System, fulfilling with the applicable requirements.

Política de Medio Ambiente

Set as Policy Environmental the implementation of a system that allows disclosure, maintenance and improvement continuously its environmental performance, in accordance with the requirements of the standard ISO 14000: 2004, with the fundamental objective of achieving a balance between the development of its activities and the environment with which it interacts, generating ecologically and socially sustainable alternatives to ensure adequate protection and conservation of Natural Resources. This Policy is in correspondence with the established by the Ministry of Construction and on this basis is committed to:

#Carry out productive management on sustainable environmental bases and cleaner productions.
#Effectively and systematically apply existing environmental laws and regulations.
#Improve services seeking continuous improvement of environmental performance and pollution prevention.
#Develop training and awareness in environmental activity for workers.
#Maintain environmental communication with customers, suppliers, the community and society in general.
#Maintain identified and updated environmental impacts.
