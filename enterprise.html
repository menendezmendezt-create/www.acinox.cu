Brief relate

Our history begins on March 4, 2003, with the name of Management and Logistics Company Technology (GLT), in 2005 the corporate purpose and the original name, adopting the name of Engineering Services Company, in its original form. abbreviated ACINOX INGENIERIA.
Finally, in 2008 the corporate purpose and the denomination are modified again, being recognized as what it is today Comprehensive Engineering and Construction Services Company.


About us

01

WHO WE ARE?

ACINOX INGENIERĺA is a Comprehensive Services Company of Engineering and Construction composed of three productive Base Business Units (UEB) and one UEB of Logistics, subordinated to the Iron and Steel Industry Business Group (GESIME) belonging to the Ministry of Industry of the Republic of Cuba.

"The satisfaction of our customers, is our Priority"

02

WHAT WE OFFER?

We specialize in offering technical services that cover the specialties of mechanics, civil construction, architecture, electricity, metallurgical processes, software, energy and planning.

UEB CIVIL WORKS

Construction, maintenance and repair of refractory, civil and industrial works.

UEB METALLIC STRUCTURES

Construction of ferrous and very diverse welded structures.

UEB ENGINEERING

Feasibility studies, preparation and administration of projects, technical assistance among others.

03

WHY CHOOSE US?

We provide a service of excellence, to meet expectations of our most demanding customers.

Our projection

MISSION

Provide integrated engineering, construction and assembly services, maintenance and repair in civil and industrial refractory works, manufacture and assembly of welded structures, parts, ferrous and non-ferrous pieces and aggregates of standard equipment or not, to achieve the full customer satisfaction with speed and professionalism, by increasing efficiency and effectiveness of our Business Management.

SOCIAL OBJECT

Provide integrated services of: Engineering, construction, assembly, maintenance and repair of refractory, civil and industrial works and manufacture and assembly of welded structures, parts, ferrous and non-ferrous pieces and aggregates of standard or non-standard equipment.

VISION

Achieve to be the consolidated Socialist State Company, distinguished of the OSDE-GESIME, with a growing social recognition inside and outside the metalworking sector in CUBA.
